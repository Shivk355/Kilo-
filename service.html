TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. UNIT COMPOSITION. The security unit to be deployed at your premises for duties may be decided with mutual consultations.

2. WORKING HOURS. Our security personnel will perform duties on working of 48 hours per week i.e. 8 hours per day and 6 days in a week. Relieving staff will be provided by us at your cost on the day when the regular staff avail their weekly off, i.e. 1/6 of the wages will be charged extra towards providing relievers.

3. SECURITY PREMISES. You will be responsible for providing and maintaining security covers like fencing walls, lights, proper gates, fire alarms, emergency light, etc. to enable us to make perfect security.

4. VALIDITY OF CONTRACT. The validity of the contract will be for one year from the date of assuming charge of duties, extendable for any further period with mutual consent.

5. TERMINATION OF CONTRACT. The contract can be terminated by either party by giving one months’ notice or payment thereof.
